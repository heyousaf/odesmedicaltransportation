Our History

Transportation Built Around the Patient

ODES Medical Transportation was established with a singular mission: to provide reliable, dignified, and compassionate non-emergency medical transportation to patients throughout the greater Los Angeles area. We recognized a critical gap in healthcare access — many patients miss vital appointments simply because they have no safe way to get there.

From our first ride, we committed to doing things differently. We invested in properly equipped vehicles, hired and trained compassionate drivers, and built scheduling systems that put patient needs first. Every process, policy, and practice at ODES is designed with one question in mind: what does the patient need?

Today, ODES serves thousands of patients from our base in Gardena, California, providing wheelchair-accessible transport, dialysis transportation, hospital discharge rides, and much more across the region.

Leadership

Meet Our Owner

Eugene Nkem Odega
Founder and Owner, ODES Medical Transportation

Eugene Nkem Odega founded ODES Medical Transportation with a vision rooted in personal conviction: that every patient, regardless of their mobility, financial situation, or circumstances, deserves safe and dependable transportation to their medical appointments.

With a background in patient care and community service, Eugene understands firsthand how critical reliable transportation is to health outcomes. He has seen too many patients miss chemotherapy sessions, dialysis appointments, and follow-up visits simply because they lacked a safe way to get there. ODES was built to change that reality.

Under Eugene's leadership, ODES has grown into a trusted name in the Gardena community and beyond. He personally oversees driver training, vehicle standards, and customer service protocols, ensuring that every rider receives the respectful, attentive care they deserve.

Eugene believes that transportation is not just a logistical service — it is an act of care. He leads ODES with that principle at the forefront, and it shows in every ride the company provides.

What We Provide

Our Transportation Services

Comprehensive non-emergency medical transportation tailored to every patient's unique needs and medical situation.

Ambulatory Transportation

Designed for patients who are mobile but require safe, reliable transport to and from medical appointments. Our drivers provide door-to-door assistance, ensuring patients are comfortable from the moment they leave home to the moment they return.

Wheelchair Transportation

Our wheelchair-accessible vehicles are specially outfitted with ramps, lifts, and secure tie-down systems to safely accommodate manual and power wheelchairs. Trained attendants assist with boarding and disembarking.

Medical Appointment Transportation

Scheduled, reliable transport to any medical appointment — primary care, specialist, dental, vision, mental health, and more. We track your appointment time and plan pickup windows strategically so you never arrive late.

Hospital Discharge Transportation

Transitioning home from a hospital stay is a vulnerable moment. Our hospital discharge service ensures patients are transported home safely, comfortably, and without delay. We coordinate directly with hospital discharge teams.

Dialysis Transportation

Dialysis patients require transportation multiple times per week, every week, without fail. We specialize in scheduled dialysis transport, building consistent, reliable routes and times around each patient's treatment schedule.

Physical Therapy Transportation

Consistent attendance at physical therapy appointments is critical to recovery. We provide dependable transport to PT sessions, helping patients complete their prescribed treatment courses without disruption.

Rehabilitation Transportation

For patients recovering from strokes, traumatic injuries, or major surgeries, ODES provides reliable transport to inpatient and outpatient rehabilitation centers, ensuring patients can access the intensive therapies that support their recovery.

Senior Transportation

Elderly patients deserve transportation that is unhurried, respectful, and attentive to their individual needs. Our senior transportation service offers extra time for boarding and alighting, with drivers trained in senior care sensitivity.

Long Distance Medical Transportation

Specialized medical facilities and research hospitals often require patients to travel significant distances. Our long-distance service extends our commitment to comfort and safety over extended journeys, with climate-controlled vehicles and attendants available when needed.
